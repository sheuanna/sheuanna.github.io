Venmo

The first time I had heard about Venmo was in 2015 when I moved to the United States. I was having dinner with a few friends and we were trying to figure out how to split the bill when one said “I’ll care of it, just Venmo me”. That evening I downloaded Venmo and was amazed at how easy it made consumer peer-to-peer payments. I have not touched my bank money transfer, physical cash, or PayPal accounts since.
