About me

I am currently an MBA candidate at the Kellogg School of Management in the accelerated 1 year program, where I am further exploring my interests in technology, innovation, and entrepreneurship. After graduation I plan to work in a high growth technology company in a product management role.

I received my undergraduate degree from the University of Toronto in Industrial Engineering where I focused on operations research, information systems, and human centred design and received the Gordon Cressy Leadership Award for outstanding contributions to the university.

I've had the opportunity to work on interesting initatives in my professional life including the launch of YouTube Red when I was a part of the strategy team at YouTube in the San Francisco Bay Area, and a paper on technological disruption as a management consultant in Deloitte Consulting's corporate strategy practice in Toronto.

I spend a significant amount of time thinking about technology as well as getting outside and exploring - I've detailed some of my thoughts on this site!
